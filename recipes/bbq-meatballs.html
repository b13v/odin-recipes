BBQ Meatballs Recipe

Description

These BBQ meatballs are a delicious and easy recipe that can be made in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, onion, tomato paste, and diced tomatoes.
  3. Shape into meatballs and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cooked through.
