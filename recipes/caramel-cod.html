Caramel Cod

Description

This caramel cod is a delicious and easy recipe that can be made in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) cod fillet
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together butter and brown sugar.
  3. Place cod fillet in a baking dish and pour butter mixture over it.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
