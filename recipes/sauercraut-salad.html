Sauerkraut Salad

Description

This sauerkraut salad is a delicious and easy recipe that can be made in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package sauerkraut
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow bell pepper

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine sauerkraut, green onions, celery, carrots, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper.
  2. Toss to combine.
